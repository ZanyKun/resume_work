Color Sample Warm, Cool, or Neutral Link to Details Page
My favorite color that I researched for Week 5 would have to be the color purple. I think the fact that the color purple can be both a warm or cool color depending on what colors are matched with it to be intriguing. With this in mind, I would be able to create either a warm or cool color scheme in my final template for Project I depending on what I am trying to go for in terms of aesthetic appeal. This allows me creative freedom during the process to see which one looks better, which I would not be able to do with just a cool color or warm color. I also really like that the color purple stands for wealth and prosperity.
