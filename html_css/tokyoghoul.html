Tokyo Ghoul


The second anime on our list is Tokyo ghoul. This anime is about a character named Ken Kaneki who barley survives an encounter with a ghoul. In the hospital, he awakens with the frightening fact that he is in fact a half ghoul and must consume flesh to survive. He is taken in by ghouls at a coffee shop and is trained to survive. He must fit in with the ghouls, and he must hide his identity as a ghoul from his human friends. Humans called investigators are tasked with hunting down all ghouls and are combat experts. Eventually the government orders a purge of all ghouls from japan and this places Ken in a very difficult situation.



Tokyo Ghoul is not for the faint of heart but thats exactly what helps makes it stand out from other animes. Other drama anime may keep their drama grounded in the real world, Tokyo Ghoul expands upon what we know of this world by adding a fantasy element to the anime.
