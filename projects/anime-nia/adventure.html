Adventure Anime

Animes with the adventure in mind!


Adventure anime is normally characterized by a quest outside our protagonists everyday life. An example of this might include being transported to another world or another time and experiencing events that would not normally occur. These situations can be dangerous in some way or perhaps mildy hazardous. A live-action series example might be Doctor Who or Once Upon a Time. Each of these demonstrate a change in everyday life and an unexpected change in setting.
